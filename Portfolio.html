Portfolio

Selected work — game prototypes, UX/UI interfaces, and interactive experiences.

Prospector

Click Here

Prospector is a card-based puzzle game inspired by TriPeaks mechanics, where players clear the board by choosing cards that are one value higher or lower than the active card. For example, a 5 can be played on either a 4 or a 6. Each choice creates a flow of strategy as players try to build long streaks, manage remaining cards, and maximize their score. I designed the visual theme, layout, and core gameplay loop, focusing on smooth card transitions and easy-to-read UI elements

Updraft

Click Here

Updraft is a fast-paced 3D flight prototype focused on smooth aerial movement and environmental traversal. Players navigate through stylized floating clouds using boost mechanics to gain speed. My work included tuning the flight physics, building the sky environment, and designing UI elements like the boost indicator to provide clear real-time feedback.

Dundgeon Delver

Click Here

Dungeon Delver is a top-down retro dungeon crawler inspired by NES-era action games. Players fight enemies in maze-like rooms using quick attacks and precise dodging. I developed the movement system, basic combat mechanics, and tile-based level design. The project emphasizes readability, old-school charm, and tight, responsive controls.

Super Space-Stronaught

Click Here

Super Space-Stronaught is a retro pixel-art platformer where players take on the role of a daring astronaut exploring an abandoned space craft. Navigate gravity-defying terrain, dodge hazards, and collect power-ups across multiple levels. The game blends classic side-scrolling action with a vibrant space aesthetic, offering a nostalgic yet fresh interstellar adventure.

Space Blaster

Click Here

A high-energy arcade shooter inspired by classics like Galaga. Players pilot a sleek spacecraft through waves of enemy formations in deep space, dodging projectiles and unleashing rapid-fire lasers. With swirling nebula backdrops and escalating difficulty, Space Blaster! delivers fast-paced reflex gameplay and satisfying shoot-'em-up thrills.
